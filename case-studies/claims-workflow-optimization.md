# Claims Workflow Optimization
**AI + Healthcare Operations Case Study**

## Problem 
Healthcare claims processing spans multiple systems and requires high-volume data handling, often relying on manual review and reconciliation. These inefficiencies drive processing delays, increase error rates, and create inconsistent outcomes across member and provider requests.

At scale, these challenges impact operational efficiency, cost, and overall service experience.

## Current Workflow
- Claims are submitted and routed through multiple systems  
- Manual review is required to resolve discrepancies  
- Teams rely on fragmented data sources  
- High volumes contribute to backlog and delays  

## Pain Points
- Repetitive manual work  
- Delayed processing times  
- Increased risk of human error  
- Limited real-time visibility into workflows  
- Inefficient case routing  

## Proposed AI Solution
- Intelligent routing of claims based on complexity and urgency  
- Automated discrepancy detection across systems  
- AI-assisted validation to reduce manual review  
- Case summarization to improve handling speed  

## Expected Impact
- Reduced processing time and backlog  
- Improved accuracy and consistency  
- Decreased manual workload  
- Faster resolution for members and providers  
- More efficient use of operational resources  

## Skills Demonstrated
- Healthcare operations analysis  
- Workflow optimization  
- AI solution design  
- Systems thinking  
- Problem-solving in high-volume environments  

## Notes
This case study is based on real-world healthcare experience and reflects common operational challenges, without including proprietary or sensitive data.

## Future Considerations
- Incorporation of predictive analytics to identify high-risk or high-complexity claims earlier in the workflow
