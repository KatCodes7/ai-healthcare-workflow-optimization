# AI-Driven Intelligent Routing System

## Problem
In high-volume healthcare operations, cases such as claims, prior authorizations, and member inquiries are often routed using static rules or manual triage. This can lead to inefficient distribution of work, delays in urgent cases, and mismatches between case complexity and staff expertise.

## Current Workflow
- Cases enter the system through multiple channels (claims, provider requests, member inquiries)  
- Routing is based on basic rules, queues, or manual assignment  
- Limited prioritization beyond general categories  
- Staff must assess complexity after receiving the case  
- High variability in handling time and outcomes

## Pain Points
- Urgent or complex cases are not consistently prioritized  
- Inefficient workload distribution across teams  
- Delays caused by misrouted or reassigned cases  
- Lack of visibility into case urgency and impact  
- Increased cognitive load for staff during triage

## Proposed AI Solution
- AI-driven routing that evaluates case complexity, urgency, and type in real time  
- Natural language processing (NLP) to analyze case notes, documentation, and request details  
- Dynamic prioritization based on member impact, deadlines, and risk factors  
- Matching cases to staff based on expertise, workload, and availability  
- Continuous learning from historical outcomes to improve routing accuracy over time

## Expected Impact
- Faster resolution of high-priority cases  
- Improved alignment between case complexity and staff expertise  
- Reduced need for reassignment and manual triage  
- Increased operational efficiency and consistency  
- Better experience for members, providers, and internal teams

## Skills Demonstrated
- Systems thinking and workflow design  
- AI application strategy (including NLP concepts)  
- Process optimization in high-volume environments  
- Healthcare operations insight  
- Data-driven decision modeling

## Notes
This case study is based on real-world healthcare experience and reflects common operational challenges, without including proprietary or sensitive data.
